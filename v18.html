PAI Launcher — How to Use

↻ Rotate ring — drag any outer project circle left or right
✥ Move window — drag the dark void between the ring and center hub
↗ Open project — click any outer circle to launch it in a new window
⬢ Center hub — click PAI, Claude, or Hermes to launch each system
⊡ Controls — hover the top edge to reveal S/M/L resize, minimize, and close
